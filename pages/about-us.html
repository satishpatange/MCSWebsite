About us

Incepted in the year 2015, Melange CS is not just a pool of experts offering designing solutions to various engineering and manufacturing processes, but a team of visionaries, ambitious, and customer-oriented people, who work round the clock to deliver timely solutions, at competitive rates. We are a growing team of 10 highly qualified professionals who offer realistic and customized engineering solutions to our clients spread across the globe. Besides building a strong client base in and around India, we’ve developed a strong customer in a lot of European countries, and as well as in the United States of America.

Our Vision

To contribute towards the engineering advancements in the human society, thereby simplifying life.

Our Mission

Melange CS aims at empowering high-quality CAD engineering solutions to its clients, to help them save time and cost, and eventually achieve their business goals.

Our Values

Customer-Centric

Whatever we do, we do it for our clients and to help them outperform their counterparts in the industry. For us, our clients are our purpose to be in the business, and helping our clients to achieve their goals, remains our objective.

Transparency

We maintain complete transparency right from the first meeting to the completion of the project. We believe in offering transparent and real-time updates to our client at every stage of the project.

Continuous Improvement

We firmly believe that continuous improvement is the key to thrive and excel in today’s competitive environment. Hence, we do not compete with our counterparts, but with ourselves to improve by the day and work tirelessly towards fulfilling our objectives.

Precision and Pace

We acknowledge the fact that the fast-paced work today needs quick, accurate and customized solutions, which is why we nurture pace or speed as one of our important values.

Honoring Commitments

We believe in honoring our commitments made to our clients, through our established technical and professional capabilities. We never let down our clients, and fulfill our commitments in a timely manner at all times.
