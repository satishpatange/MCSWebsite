Reverse Engineering

Our product development cycle services proposition involves vital reverse engineering services as well. This helps our clients to analyze peer products and also make improvements to it while redeveloping it with us. At Mélange CS, we follow a very organized and well-defined process to perform reverse engineering, which involves the following procedures.

  • Search for CAD data that captures the original design intent of a product or an object.
  • Design a new part that fits to a legacy one.
  • Model performance surfaces with precision.
  • Update the CAD models of your tooling to align them with the shop-floor changes.
  • Redesign the part in a way that it won’t encounter any manufacturing issues or defects.
  • Reinvent or modernize your manufacturing process.

We offer the following reverse engineering services to our clients, based on their requirements.

  • Non-contact inspection
  • CMM scanning
  • 3D laser scanning
  • CGI Scanning
  • Computer Aided Inspection and Validation
  • Part redesigning
