Oil and Gas

Oil and gas systems require an extremely carefully planned and developed design to ensure that the systems and the subsequent processes are carried out with optimal safety and efficiency. At Melange CS, we offer a wide range of oil and gas system designing services. We offer a lot of onsite oil and gas system designing services to oil refineries, natural gas processing units, petrochemical units. Our team of expert design professionals ensures designs that deliver maximum output. Our broad range of oil and gas design systems entails the following.

  • Process Engineering
  • Mechanical
  • Materials
  • Pipeline – static and dynamic modeling
  • Structural
  • Instrumentation
  • Subsea
  • Automation
