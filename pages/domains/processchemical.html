Process/Chemical Industry

The chemical industry, owing to the use of hazardous chemicals that, in turn, involve a lot of cost, demand for a well-designed plant as well as internal process design that ensures prudent utilization of resources, and responds in the expected way to critical concerns such as non-linear increment in surface area to mass, fluid dynamics, reaction kinetics, etc. Besides, the chemical industry is comprised of several compliances, adherence to which is imperative at all times. Nevertheless, as it begins from the designing process, our team at Melange CS ensures an extremely well-planned, careful and compliant design to ensure that future concerns are taken care of in the initial phases itself. The use of latest designing techniques and tools helps us be in a better position to design processes, heavy equipment, plant layout in the most appropriate and accurate manner.

  • Process Flow Diagram
  • Utility requirements
  • P & ID
  • Specifications of equipment and bought out items
  • Plant/equipment layout with load data (Plan and elevation)
  • Structural designs
  • Fabrication drawing for equipment
  • Piping GA
  • Piping isometrics
  • Instruments and Electricals detailing
